About Us


The first plataform which help companies to give travel perks to all their employees.

LeisureBonus was launched in June 2019, is plataform focusing on helping other companies to give benefits to their employees to reward them for their work. Founded in Portugal, Lisbon currenctly operates in Portugal, but also expanding globally.

LeisureBonus is always free for costumers and we never charge any travel commission. We work directly with costumers's companies who pay us a comission for using our plataform.
