To create an account:

  1. Head to the LeisureBonus website and click on 'Register' in the top right corner.
  2. Enter the information required for your company then click 'Continue'. For most companies, this will be a corporate email or employee ID.
  3. Complete the rest of the required information and then 'Create Account'.

Please remember that LeisureBonus is only available for corporate users, you will need a CODE which is provided for your company.

If you can't remember your password you can always request a reset from the login page:

  1. Head to the login page and enter your email.
  2. On the password page select 'Forgot your password?' from the options underneath.
  3. Enter the email linked to your LeisureBonus account and you'll then receive an email with a link to reset your password.

Remember your password must be at least 8 characters with at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter and either numbers or symbols.
